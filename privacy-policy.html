Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Calvy stores all your personal data entirely on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information.

Last updated: March 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Calvy ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application Calvy (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Calvy stores all personal data exclusively on your iPhone using local storage. This includes:

  • Your profile information (age, height, weight, activity level, goals)
  • Daily food logs and calorie entries
  • Weight log entries
  • Water intake records
  • Streak and progress data
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

2.2 Food Photos for Analysis

When you use the photo scanning feature, your food photos are transmitted to our secure processing service (Supabase Edge Function) which forwards them to the OpenAI Vision API for nutritional analysis. Photos are processed in real time and immediately discarded — they are never stored on our servers or any third-party servers.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not require account registration
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use advertising trackers or analytics SDKs
  • We do not sell your data to third parties

3. How We Use Your Information

The food photos you submit are used solely to provide you with nutritional estimates (calories, protein, carbs, fat). The analysis results are returned to your device and stored locally. We do not retain, analyze, or use your photos or food data for any other purpose.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 OpenAI

Food photos are processed by OpenAI's Vision API to identify food items and estimate nutritional content. OpenAI processes these images in accordance with their privacy policy. Photos are not retained by OpenAI beyond the immediate processing request. For more information, visit openai.com/privacy.

4.2 Supabase

We use Supabase Edge Functions as a secure proxy to forward photo analysis requests to OpenAI. Supabase does not store your food photos. For more information, visit supabase.com/privacy.

4.3 RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect transaction data related to your subscription purchases. This data is used solely for subscription management and is not linked to your personal health data. For more information, visit revenuecat.com/privacy.

4.4 Apple

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. Apple's privacy practices are governed by the Apple Privacy Policy.

5. Permissions

Calvy requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: Used to photograph food for calorie and nutritional analysis. Photos are processed for analysis and immediately discarded.
  • Photo Library: Used to select existing photos of food for nutritional analysis.
  • Notifications: Used to send optional meal reminders and streak notifications. You can enable or disable these at any time in the app settings.

6. Data Security

Since your personal data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security features (such as Face ID, Touch ID, or passcode). We do not have access to your device or your locally stored data.

Photo transmissions to our analysis service are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

7. Children's Privacy

Calvy is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

Since all your personal data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can:

  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Reset your profile data within the app settings
  • Disable notifications at any time in the app or your device settings

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
